About Us

Department of IT

Welcome to Emitrix, the flagship technical event hosted by the Department of Information Technology at Pragati Engineering College. Emitrix stands as a beacon of excellence, drawing B.Tech students from around the globe to a dynamic platform designed for the exchange of knowledge and ideas in the rapidly evolving field of Information Technology.

At Emitrix, we take pride in curating an immersive experience that transcends geographical boundaries, fostering a community where innovation thrives and boundaries are pushed. Our mission is to empower students with opportunities to showcase their talents, engage with industry experts, and explore the forefront of technological innovation.

For years, Emitrix has served as a catalyst for intellectual growth and professional development, offering participants a unique blend of interactive sessions, hands-on workshops, and engaging discussions. Whether you're a seasoned technologist or a budding enthusiast, Emitrix welcomes you to join us on a journey of exploration, discovery, and collaboration.

Join us as we embark on a quest to redefine the landscape of Information Technology, one idea at a time. Together, let's inspire, innovate, and ignite the future of technology.

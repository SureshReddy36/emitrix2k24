Our Events

Explore Our Events

CodeMaze

Code Maze is a dynamic debugging event where participants receive error-ridden code snippets and race against the clock to identify and rectify issues. From syntax errors to logic flaws, participants navigate the coding maze, showcasing their troubleshooting skills. This challenge promotes collaboration, enhances coding proficiency, and provides a thrilling platform for participants to sharpen their problem-solving abilities in real-time.

WebMosaic

WebMosaic is not just an event; it's a canvas waiting for your creative strokes. Participants will be given raw HTML code, and the challenge is to elevate it into a visually stunning web masterpiece through your styling wizardry. From fonts to colors, layouts to animations, the power to design is in your hands!.

ProductPalooza

Product Palooza is a fast-paced marketing challenge where participants are handed random technical products and tasked with creating on-the-spot pitches. From gadgets to software, they showcase creativity by highlighting key features and addressing target audiences, fostering collaboration in a competitive environment. The event is a dynamic platform that hones marketing skills, making it a fun and engaging experience for participants to master the art of spontaneous product promotion.

StackTheCode

Stack the Code' is not your ordinary coding challenge; it's a fusion of analysis, prediction, and a touch of strategic brilliance. Participants will be presented with intriguing code snippets, and their task is to decipher the output. But here's the twist – your coding acumen determines the arrangement of cups, and the strategic placement could lead you to triumph!.
